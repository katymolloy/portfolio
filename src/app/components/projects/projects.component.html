Style and Shine

This website was created for a friend of mine's dog grooming business. During it's creation, I made sure to first establish what she wanted out of her business website. I offered different solutions, but in the end we decided to keep it simple as a static website, highlighting her services, experience and skills. The design of the website is mobile-first, as the majority of her clients would be viewing the website on mobile. All branding graphics were also created by me.
